I’m Anais NG, a Mauritian full-time illustrator and graphic designer.

I primarily focus on creating engaging educational content for children, combining creativity with a love for storytelling to inspire young minds.

In addition to illustration, I’m skilled in coding and video editing, which allows me to bring a versatile approach to my creative projects. While my work often centers around children's content, I have a soft spot for manga, drawing inspiration from its dynamic style and captivating narratives to add a unique flair to my art.
